About Us



Lets Build Your Dream Together

Welcome to Bakangizo & Partners, we are an architectural firm that deals with designing, building, selling of building materials and training of young architects. Here at Bakangizo & Partners we care about the environment and safety of those who work on our sites. At Bakangizo & Partners we dont just build houses, we turn dreams into reality.

Meet the CEO



Born in January of 1975, Architect Farouk Dansabo is the founder and CEO of Bakangizo & Partners, he studied Architecture in university. Mr Farouk Dansabo said " As a trained architect, it has always been my dream to one day build a company that will be to design, construct, sell building materials and train young architects." And he did just that, having always had a passion for the profession, he knew early on that was the carrer path he wanted to pursue. Now he helps others turn their dream designs into reality.
